Trailblazer

In Honkai: Star Rail, the Trailblazer is the playable main protagonist.They are woken during the game's opening sequence by Kafka and Silver Wolf, who then leave them on the Herta Space Station for March 7th and Dan Heng to find after the Antimatter Legion attack. The player gets to select either Stelle or Caelus, along with their Recptacle Codename.

Playable Characters

March 7th

An enthusiastic girl who was once saved by the Astral Express from enternal ice. Now, whenever she goes on a trip, she always brings her camera in the hopes of getting a photo of something from her past.

Dan Heng

The Astral Express's cold and reserved train guard. He joined the Express crew to get away from his isolated past and wields a spear named Cloud-Piercer.

Himeko

She is an adventurous scientist who, as a young girl, came upon and fixed a broken train, and who now travels the universe with the Astral Express.

Welt

A veteran crew member of the Astral Express and former ruler of Anti-Entropy who has prevented the extinction of Earth. He inherited the word "Welt," which means world.
