"An individual's fate shouldn't be limited to a single path ordained by heaven."

Role-playing gacha video game Honkai: Star Rail was created by miHoYo and distributed globally by its subsidiary HoYoverse. On April 26, 2023, it became available for Windows and mobile platforms. Honkai: Star Rail features open-world and dungeon exploration, as well as a number of mechanics that were carried over from HoYoverse's action role-playing game Genshin Impact, including the gacha system. Players build up a team of four characters and command them in turn-based combat. Come aboard with us on the Astral Express, Trailblazers!
